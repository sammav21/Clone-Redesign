Our Story


The Yo-Burger concept was first born on a vacation trip to California. With the abundance of fast burger restaurants on the West Coast, many days were spent indulging in delicious, juicy burgers while enjoying the sunshine. We quickly noticed a pattern though in our behaviorâ€¦ we craved the cool, natural flavor of frozen yogurt to wash it down. After returning to New York City, we continued to search for burgers followed by frozen yogurt. The Yo-Burger experience was officially born.

Yo-Burger is committed to providing the highest quality products on the market. Our Black Angus beef is provided fresh daily, and all burgers are made to order with your choice of toppings. All of our frozen yogurt is non-fat or low-fat and made with all natural ingredients. Our yogurt is filled with live and active yogurt cultures which help maintain healthy digestive and immune systems. Our yogurt is also a good source of calcium which helps support strong bones. All of our yogurt is certified kosher.
